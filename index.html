Complete Guide

Understanding testing procedures, queue systems, and gamemode specs.

⚙️
Getting Started
1
Turn on "Show All Channels" by clicking Silent Tiers in the top left corner
2
Scroll down and go to request-test
3
Click Register / Update Profile
4
Choose Crystal PvP to be added to the waitlist
5
Wait patiently until a tester is available and opens queue
📋
Queue System
  • Being in the waitlist means you will be tested at some point. Please be patient.
  • Join the queue when a tester pings with a button — it's for players ready now.
  • If no testers are available, there is no open queue message.
  • Queues close when no testers remain active. Your position is not saved.
  • Press leave if you no longer want to be tested.
  • 12-hour cooldown after each test before you can rejoin.

Definitions & Terms
Equal Ping

HT

LT

FT
First to (e.g., FT10)
💎
Crystal PvP
  • End Crystal combat on 1.21+ servers
  • Players tested on crystal placement and detonation ability
  • Anchor mechanics, placement speed, and totem management evaluated
  • Tests on equal ping EU servers
  • Tiers: LT5 (lowest) to HT1 (highest)
