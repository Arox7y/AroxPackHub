What Minecraft versions are supported?
+
All Arox packs support Minecraft 1.8 through the latest version. Whether you're playing on 1.8 PvP servers or modern survival — the packs are compatible. Each pack page shows the exact version range.

Yes! All Arox packs are optimized for performance. Lower resolution textures (16x) especially give a significant FPS boost. Even 32x packs are built lean. Want extra performance? Check out Arox Tweaks.
